MY FAVOURITE DRIVER

Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula One driver born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England. He entered F1 in 2007 and immediately impressed the world with his talent, winning his first World Championship just one year later. Over the years he has become one of the most successful drivers in history, earning seven World Drivers’ Championships and setting multiple records, including the most pole positions and podium finishes. Hamilton is known for his incredible racecraft, determination, and ability to deliver when it matters most. Beyond his achievements on track, he has used his platform to advocate for diversity, equality, and environmental initiatives. His impact reaches far beyond motorsport, establishing him as both a racing legend and a positive force for change in the world.

My Favourite Team: Ferrari

Ferrari is more than just a Formula 1 team — it’s a symbol of racing heritage, passion, and excellence. With over 240 race victories and 16 Constructors' Championships, Ferrari remains the most successful and iconic team in the history of Formula 1.

In 2024, I had the amazing opportunity to attend the Monza Grand Prix in Italy — the home race for Ferrari. The atmosphere was electrifying, filled with the energy of the “Tifosi” fans waving red flags and cheering for their heroes. Watching Charles Leclerc win at Monza was an unforgettable experience — seeing the red sea of Ferrari supporters celebrate was something truly magical.
